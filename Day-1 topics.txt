### Why this design works for corporate manufacturing training

This approach uses **one integrated manufacturing dataset** throughout the session, allowing participants to solve different business problems with the same data. This mirrors how analytics teams work in real factories and helps learners understand that the choice of algorithm depends on the business objective—not on switching datasets. It also provides a natural progression from traditional machine learning techniques to ANN-based predictive maintenance, making the session highly relevant for Industry 4.0 and Smart Manufacturing initiatives.
